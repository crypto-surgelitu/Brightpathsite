About Us

Welcome to Brightpath-digital awareness workshop

BrightPath is a one-day practical skills workshop designed specifically for small business owners. In today's digital age, having a strong online presence is crucial for the success of any business. This workshop aims to equip small business owners with the essential skills and knowledge needed to navigate the digital landscape effectively.

During the workshop, participants will learn the basics of digital marketing, including how to create and manage social media accounts, develop a website, and utilize online advertising to reach their target audience. The workshop will also cover important topics such as online safety, data privacy, and cybersecurity to ensure that small business owners can protect their businesses from potential threats.

In addition to marketing and safety, the workshop will introduce participants to low-cost digital tools

Required tools

  • Laptop
  • Smartphone
  • Internet connection
  • Notebook and pen

That can help streamline their operations and improve efficiency. From project management software to accounting tools, participants will discover a range of resources that can help them run their businesses more effectively without breaking the bank.
